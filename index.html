A Humble Dedication Page to One of The Greatest Films of All Time:

Honestly, every time I ask anyone about this movie, I normally get a
head scratch as a respone. Brimming with unexpected talent and
full of moments that have become a part of our culture, Freaks is
a must view for anyone who likes a good revenge tale.

Mainly centered on the story of Hans, played beautifully by
Harry Earles, and his love and eventual marriage to the beautiful
Cleopatra(played by Olga Baclanova). Unknowkn to Hans, Cleopatra
only has eyes for Hans's inheritance and she teams up with Hercules
(played by Henry Victor) the strongman to get their grubby mitts on
his money.
